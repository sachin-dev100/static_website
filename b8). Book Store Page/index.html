Wings of Fire

by Arun Tiwari

Every common man who by his sheer grit and hard work achieve success should share his story with the rest for they man find inspiration and strength to go on, in his story. The 'Wings of fire' is one such Autobiography by visionary scientists Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam.who from very humble beginnings rose to be the president of india. The book is full of insight, personal moments and life experiences of Dr. Kalam. It gives us an understanding on his journey of success.
